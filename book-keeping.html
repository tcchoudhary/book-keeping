Morton, Watson & Young is a one hundred year old Melbourne firm with a reputation for outstanding service, value and integrity. We have proudly practiced under the one name since 1912, providing over a century of service to our clients, a unique achievement in the business community.

In September 1997, our 85th anniversary, Morton Watson and Young moved into new purpose-built premises in the Dandenong CBD and combined its Melbourne, Berwick and Dandenong offices into the new location.
